Black-eyed Peas & Sushi Rice

The big takeaway from this dish is that good beans do not come from a can. You've got to soak them. There's a big difference.

Required

  • Sticky/sushi rice
  • 3 cups of black-eyed peas with some of the water it cooked in
  • A head of bok choy (or some type of chard), chopped
  • 5 cloves of Garlic, sliced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 red pepper, chopped
  • A few tbsp of ex vg olive oil
  • Paprika
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt & Pepper

Instruction

  1. Start cooking the rice
  2. Heat frying pan over medium high heat
  3. Add olive oil and garlic and brown
  4. Add garlic and saut√© for a few minutes
  5. Add everything else and lower heat to a simmer for at least 45 minutes
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Serve over rice
