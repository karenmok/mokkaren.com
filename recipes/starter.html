Sourdough Starter

To make sourdough starter, you'll need a resting place about 70 to 80 degrees and a mason jar.

Required

  • Water
  • Unbleached All-Purpose Flour

Instruction

Increments will always be 50g of flour and 50g of water so a scale is required.

Day 0: Init

Initial mix. Set aside with lid loosely on top.

+48 hours: Bubbles

Feed by stiring in flour and water.

+24 hours: More bubbles

Half & Feed.

+24 hours: Bubbles and pretty stinky

Half & Feed.

+24: Most likely ready

Feed. At this point I'll feed and not half because I'll want to use some the next day.

Maintenance

If outside the fridge, half & feed daily. If in the fridge, half & feed weekly. Remove hooch as necessary.
